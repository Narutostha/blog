Cyber Security

What Is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information; extorting money from users via ransomware; or interrupting normal business processes. Implementing effective cybersecurity measures is particularly challenging today because there are more devices than people, and attackers are becoming more innovative.

Stay Secure Online: Essential Cybersecurity Tips

Maintaining cyber security requires a vigilant effort on your part. This includes using complex and unique passwords for all accounts, with the help of a password manager for secure storage. Two-factor authentication (2FA) should be enabled whenever possible to add an extra layer of security during logins. Keeping your operating system, applications, and web browsers up-to-date is crucial as updates often contain security patches. Be cautious of phishing attempts that try to trick you into clicking suspicious links or attachments, even if they appear legitimate.
